Orlando FC, based in East Kallada, Kerala, is a vibrant and dynamic football club established in 2024.
The club was formed with a vision to promote football at the grassroots level and provide a platform for young and talented players in the region to showcase their skills.
Driven by passion and teamwork, Orlando FC has quickly become a prominent name in the local football scene, inspiring a new generation of athletes.

The team stands out not only for its competitive spirit but also for its strong sense of community.
Orlando FC is more than just a football club—it’s a family that brings people together,
fostering sportsmanship and camaraderie among players and fans alike.
With a focus on discipline, dedication, and consistent training, the club aims
to make a significant mark in Kerala's football circuit and beyond.

As a team founded on dreams and determination, Orlando FC is committed to nurturing talent and achieving excellence on and off the field.
With its inaugural year already sparking excitement in East Kallada,
the club is set to grow into a powerhouse of football talent,
making its mark in tournaments and earning the respect of football enthusiasts across the state.
